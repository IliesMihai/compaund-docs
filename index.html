Privacy Policy

Compaund — Build habits that stick

Effective date: March 4, 2026

Compaund is developed by Ampip. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data the app handles and how it is used.

Data That Stays on Your Device

All habit data you create in Compaund — including habits, completions, streaks, journal entries, and identities — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not operate servers that collect or store your data.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to sign in with your Apple ID, your data is synced across your devices using Apple's CloudKit service. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Sign in with Apple (Optional)

If you use Sign in with Apple, we receive only the information Apple provides: your user identifier, and optionally your name and email address. This data is stored locally on your device (in the Keychain) and is used solely to personalize your experience within the app. We do not share this information with any third parties.

Apple Health (Optional)

Compaund can optionally write habit completion data to Apple Health (e.g., workouts, mindful minutes, water intake). This requires your explicit permission. Health data is handled entirely by Apple's HealthKit framework and is never sent to us or any third party.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, they are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Analytics and Tracking

Compaund does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking tools. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or behavioral analytics.

Data You Can Export or Delete

  • Export: You can export all your data in CSV or JSON format from the app's Profile settings.
  • Delete: You can delete all your data from within the app at any time via Profile > Manage Data.
  • Uninstall: Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. iCloud data can be managed through your iCloud settings.

Children's Privacy

Compaund is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
